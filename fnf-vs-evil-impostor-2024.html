FNF VS Evil Impostor (2024)

FNF VS Evil Impostor (2024) is a thrilling multi-Week mod for Friday Night Funkin' that flips the script on FNF VS Impostor. This mod introduces a fresh set of foes, departing from the familiar Red, Green, and Black. Get ready to confront their mirrored adversaries, including Mint, Hot Pink, and White, along with a diverse cast of characters.

Spanning 19 original songs, FNF VS Evil Impostor (2024) offers a challenging gameplay experience. Players can immerse themselves in a whole new Henry Stickmin Week, adding an exciting twist to the game.

With its unique storyline and engaging music, this mod offers a fresh take on the Friday Night Funkin' universe. Dive into the rhythm, master the beats, and conquer the new challenges that await in FNF VS Evil Impostor (2024)!
