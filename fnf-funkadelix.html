Friday Night Funkin' FUNKADELIX

Friday Night Funkin' FUNKADELIX is a vibrant remix mod that takes the rhythm game to a whole new level of funkiness. Created by a talented team including Paciofd, JamsDX, eggcat, isophoro, Krizbiee, and RecD, this mod reimagines every song with fresh character sprites and powerful new lyrics.

Step into the groove as you face off against a lineup of colorful characters, each with their own unique style and personality. From the smooth moves of the protagonist to the funky beats of the opponents, every moment is filled with energy and excitement.

The gameplay remains as addictive as ever, challenging players to hit the right notes in time with the music. But with the added flair of FUNKADELIX, each track feels like a fresh experience, keeping players on their toes and eager for more.

Get ready to immerse yourself in a world of rhythm, style, and funk with Friday Night Funkin' FUNKADELIX. Let's go!
