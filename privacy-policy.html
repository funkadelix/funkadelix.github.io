Privacy Policy

Funkadelix Games is deeply committed to your privacy. The following outlines our ongoing dedication to protecting our users’ privacy.

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information from our users.

Members

Members may use their email addresses to sign up. We do not store or share these email addresses with any third parties.

External Links

This site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these sites.

Third-Party Advertising

We use third-party advertisements that may use your cookies to serve ads. We have opted out of using cookies to display advertisements on our site. However, if you live in a country with GDPR regulations or in California under CCPA, we have automatically opted out with our third-party providers from using your cookies. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out. We pledge that we will never have pop-up ads on Funkadelix Games. We understand how annoying they can be.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@funkadelix.github.io

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
